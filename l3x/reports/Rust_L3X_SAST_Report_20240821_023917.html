High Severity Issues:

  1. Arithmetic Overflow/Underflow (RUST012, RUST021):

  1. Unwrapping Issues (RUST002, RUST024, Rust-Solana002):

  2. Array Access Without Boundary Checks (Rust-Solana007):

    • Instances (lines 17, 18, 34, 62) that access arrays or vectors without ensuring valid indices.

Low Severity Issues:

  1. Clone Performance Issues (Rust-Solana008):
    • Lines 61, 121 highlight potential performance problems due to cloning large data structures.
